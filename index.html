About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at AstraZeneca working at the intersection of machine learning, genomics, and statistical modeling.

My research focuses on large-scale genomic analysis, multi-omics integration, and developing machine learning frameworks for uncovering biological mechanisms in population-scale datasets.

I completed my PhD at EPFL, where I developed cgNA+, a physics-informed coarse-grained model for sequence-dependent mechanics of nucleic acids trained on large-scale molecular dynamics simulations.

I also work on machine learning for musculoskeletal (MSK) modeling, enabling real-time predictions for clinical and sports applications.
